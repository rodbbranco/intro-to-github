Cook Lasagna Like a Real Developer.

When coding for hours on end, every developer needs a hearty and delicious break.
And what better way to refuel than with a warm and comforting serving of lasagna?
This recipe is not your average, run-of-the-mill lasagna.
Oh no, this is a special lasagna, cooked up by a skilled developer with a love for both technology and Italian cuisine.
So sit back, relax, and get ready for a meal that will not only satisfy your taste buds but also bring a smile to your face.
Let's get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz) of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 large container (15 oz) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
